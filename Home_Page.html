   What is the Video Game Museum?

   The Video Game Museum is a chronological chronicling of the history of Video Games, from the humble pong to the 9th generation of consoles, dot matrix to ray tracing, from 1958 al the way up to the present day, if you're interested in the surprisingly long and underated history of video games, you're in the right place.

   Ok, but where do I start?

   Well, the navigation bar on the right has every topic split nicely into console generations. If you're not sure in which Generation the topic you're interested in falls, or you just want to find out more about a topic in particular the timetable underneath should get you straight to what you're interested in.
